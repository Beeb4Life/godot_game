## Day 2 – Choosing the Game Type

### Decision
I decided to build a 2D game.

### Reasoning
- 3D games require complex modeling, rendering, and more system resources
- 2D development is faster and more manageable
- Easier handling of assets such as sprites and audio
- Allows better focus on gameplay and story

### Objectives
- Keep the project lightweight
- Ensure the game can be completed within the deadline

### Next Steps
- Finalize the story
- Start thinking about gameplay mechanics and visual style
